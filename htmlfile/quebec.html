Quebec City

Discover the architecture and European charm of this cradle of the Francophonie while indulging in the typically Nordic activities that this city abounds.


Sleep at the one and only ice hotel in North America, visit Montmorency Falls and enjoy the breathtaking views from its cable car,
try ice canoeing, take a dog sled ride and finish your day at a Nordic spa, one of the favorite winter activities of Canadians.

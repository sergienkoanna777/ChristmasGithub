Treehouse

Immerse yourself in the atmosphere of your childhood dreams by spending a night or two in a mountain
hut in the heart of a snowy forest under a starry sky.
Wood-fired, the refuge offers rustic comfort and the fresh scent of pine in abundance.
After a walk along a path that winds beneath the majestic crowns,
gather around a crackling fire to share a great meal and unforgettable stories.

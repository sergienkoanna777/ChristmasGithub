Upper Canada Village

Fall under the spell of this open-air museum located in Morrisburg, Ontario, (Canada),
which represents a 19th century village in Upper Canada!

Illuminated by more than a million lights on the occasion of Christmas,
the village offers a variety of activities for the whole family including carriage rides,
entertainment by disguised characters and old-fashioned tales around a fire.

Sweets and hot drinks will be served throughout the village.
